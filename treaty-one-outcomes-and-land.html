What were some things that were promised in the Treaty?


Some promises made were:

  • It was promised that each community of Indigenous Peoples (band) would get a reserve (a promised peice of land that belongs to the Indigenous Peoples, it is where many live) large enough to provide 160 acres of land for every family of 5. Families that were smaller got less land and larger families got more land.
  • The band of Yellow Quill got an extra 25 square miles of land that was laid out around the reserve.
  • Indigenous Peoples were not allowed to use alcohol on the reserve.
  • The Canadian Government agreed to keep a school running on the reserves if the Indigenous Peoples of that reserve wanted.
  • Each Indigenous Persons recieved a gratuity which is like a gift of $3. They also recieved $3 worth of useful stuff regularily unless the persons chooses to recieve it in cash (money).

What is the difference between Treaty one and the rest? How does it look like today?


Treaty one stands out from the other treaties because of this

  1. Treaty One was the first Treaty numbered treaty to be made.
  2. This Treaty has much less promises in it than the ones made after it.
  3. The Treaty annuity that was just explained was only $3, the other Treaties had $5.
